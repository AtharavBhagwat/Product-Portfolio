My Product Process

My approach to product management combines data-driven decision making with user-centered design thinking. Here's how I tackle product challenges:

1

Discovery & Research

I begin with comprehensive market research and user interviews to understand pain points, competitive landscape, and opportunities for innovation.

2

Analysis & Strategy

Using data analysis and strategic frameworks, I identify key metrics, evaluate product-market fit, and develop a clear roadmap aligned with business goals.

3

Design & Prototyping

I collaborate with design and engineering teams to create wireframes, prototypes, and iterative mockups based on user journey mapping and feedback.

4

Development & Testing

I facilitate cross-functional collaboration during development, conduct A/B testing, and ensure technical implementation aligns with the product vision.

5

Launch & GTM Strategy

I develop comprehensive go-to-market strategies, coordinate launch activities, and ensure smooth deployment with minimal friction for users.

6

Measurement & Iteration

I establish key performance indicators, analyze user metrics, and lead continuous improvement initiatives based on data-driven insights.
